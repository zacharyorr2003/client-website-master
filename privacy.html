Cookie & Tracking Practices

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience on our website. Cookies are small data files stored on your device.

  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or be notified when a new cookie is set. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
