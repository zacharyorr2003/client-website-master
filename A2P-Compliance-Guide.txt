A2P 10DLC COMPLIANCE GUIDE
For use with HighLevel (GoHighLevel) A2P Registration
================================================================

⚠️  HOW TO USE THIS GUIDE
This guide is written so that any person or AI assistant can
complete a full A2P 10DLC registration for any business from
start to finish without errors. All company and contact
information must be pulled from the CLIENT INTAKE FORM before
filling out anything in HighLevel. Replace every [placeholder]
with the matching field from the intake form. Follow every
section in order. Links must be live and publicly accessible
before submitting.

PLACEHOLDERS USED IN THIS GUIDE:
  [Business Name]     — Legal company name (e.g. "Acme LLC")
  [EIN]               — Employer Identification Number
  [owner email]       — Owner/primary contact email
  [support email]     — Public-facing support email
  [support phone]     — Public-facing support phone number
  [website URL]       — Full website URL with https://
  [opt-in form URL]   — Direct URL to the opt-in/contact page
  [privacy policy URL]— Direct URL to the privacy policy page
  [terms URL]         — Direct URL to the terms of service page
  [business address]  — Full street address, city, state, zip
  [rep name]          — Authorized representative full name
  [rep title]         — Authorized representative job title
  [governing state]   — State whose law governs the agreement

----------------------------------------------------------------
WHY THESE THREE PAGES MATTER
----------------------------------------------------------------
The A2P compliance scanner crawls the Opt-In Form URL and
verifies that:
  1. The opt-in form is visible in HTML (not hidden in a modal)
  2. A Privacy Policy link is present and accessible
  3. A Terms of Service link is present and accessible
  4. Consent checkboxes are unchecked by default
  5. Required disclaimer language is present

All three pages must pass before your campaign is approved.


----------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 1 REQUIREMENTS — OPT-IN FORM
----------------------------------------------------------------

The form must include TWO SEPARATE optional checkboxes —
one for Customer Care, one for Marketing. Neither can be
pre-checked. Neither can be required. The user must be able
to submit the form without checking either box.

CHECKBOX 1 — Customer Care (Transactional) — EXACT TEXT:
  "By providing a telephone number, clicking this button, and
  submitting the form, you are consenting to be contacted by
  SMS text message from [Business Name], regarding Customer
  Care (transactional). Message frequency may vary. Up to 4
  msgs/month. Message & data rates apply. Reply STOP to
  unsubscribe from further messaging from [Business Name].
  Reply HELP for more information. See our Privacy Policy
  (containing our SMS Terms) at the bottom of the page for
  more information."

CHECKBOX 2 — Marketing (Promotional) — EXACT TEXT:
  "By providing a telephone number, clicking this button, and
  submitting the form, you are consenting to be contacted by
  SMS text message from [Business Name], regarding new offers
  (promotional/marketing). Message frequency may vary. Up to
  4 msgs/month. Message & data rates apply. Reply STOP to
  unsubscribe from further messaging from [Business Name].
  Reply HELP for more information. See our Privacy Policy
  (containing our SMS Terms) at the bottom of the page for
  more information."

FORM DISCLAIMER — Must appear below the checkboxes:
  "Consent is provided exclusively for [Business Name] to
  contact the user based on the selection, not any other
  third parties mentioned on the site. SMS opt-in data is not
  shared/sold to third parties for promotional/marketing
  purposes. SMS consent is not required as a condition of
  purchase. You must be 18 or older to consent. Msg & data
  rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for support."

FOOTER LINKS — Must appear at the bottom of the page:
  - Link to Privacy Policy (opens in new tab)
  - Link to Terms & Conditions (opens in new tab)

PAGE 2 REQUIREMENTS — PRIVACY POLICY
----------------------------------------------------------------

The Privacy Policy must include ALL of the following sections.
Missing any single item can cause the compliance scan to fail.

REQUIRED SECTIONS:

1. WHAT INFORMATION IS COLLECTED
   - List all data types: name, email, phone number, etc.

2. HOW USER DATA IS USED
   - List all purposes data is used for

3. SMS OPT-IN DETAILS (Critical — scanner checks for this)
   - Describe the two opt-in types (Customer Care / Marketing)
   - State message frequency for each ("up to 4 msgs/month")
   - State that opting in is voluntary and not required
   - State age restriction: must be 18 or older to consent
   - Include STOP keyword instruction
   - Include HELP keyword instruction
   - Include "Msg & data rates may apply"

4. COOKIE & TRACKING PRACTICES
   - Describe types of cookies used (essential, analytics,
     marketing)
   - Explain how users can manage cookie preferences

5. DATA SECURITY AND HANDLING
   - Explain how user data is protected

6. USER RIGHTS
   - Right to access their data
   - Right to correct inaccurate data
   - Right to request deletion
   - Right to unsubscribe from SMS (STOP) and email
   - Contact email for rights requests

7. STRICT PROHIBITION CLAUSE
   Must state that [Business Name] is NOT affiliated with
   any lead network or third-party lead aggregator, and does
   not buy, sell, or broker leads.

8. MOBILE INFO SHARING STATEMENT — EXACT LANGUAGE REQUIRED:
   "No mobile information will be shared with third parties
   or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes."

   "All the above categories exclude text messaging originator
   opt-in data and consent; this information will not be
   shared with any third parties under any circumstances."

9. CROSS-LINK TO TERMS & CONDITIONS
   - Must include a clickable link to the Terms of Service page

PRIVACY POLICY CHECKLIST:

  [ ] Mobile info sharing statement — exact language above

PAGE 3 REQUIREMENTS — TERMS OF SERVICE
----------------------------------------------------------------

The Terms of Service must include ALL of the following clauses.

REQUIRED CLAUSES:

1. BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
   - State [Business Name] by name
   - Briefly describe the business and services offered

2. DESCRIPTION OF SMS USE CASES
   - Explicitly list the SMS use cases: Customer Care
     (transactional) and Marketing (promotional)
   - Describe what each type of message may include
     (e.g., appointment reminders, offers, promotions)

3. MESSAGING CONSENT LANGUAGE
   - Must state that submitting the form does NOT equal consent
   - Consent must come from explicitly checking the checkbox
   - Example: "Simply providing your phone number or submitting
     a form does not constitute consent to receive SMS messages
     from [Business Name]."

4. MESSAGE TYPES & FREQUENCY
   - List all message types (Customer Care / Marketing)
   - Disclose frequency: "Message frequency varies. You may
     receive up to 4 messages per month."

5. OPT-OUT INSTRUCTIONS
   - "Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe."
   - Confirm that a one-time confirmation will be sent

6. CUSTOMER SUPPORT CONTACT
   - "Reply HELP for assistance."
   - Include support email: [support email]
   - Include support phone: [support phone]

7. MESSAGE & DATA RATES DISCLOSURE
   - "Message and data rates may apply."

8. CARRIER LIABILITY DISCLAIMER
   - "Wireless carriers are not liable for delayed or
     undelivered messages."

9. AGE RESTRICTION
   - "You must be at least 18 years of age to consent to
     receive SMS messages from [Business Name]."

10. GOVERNING LAW
    - State that [governing state] law governs the agreement

11. CROSS-LINK TO PRIVACY POLICY
    - Must include a clickable link to the Privacy Policy page

TERMS OF SERVICE CHECKLIST:
  [ ] Business name and description
  [ ] Description of SMS use cases (Customer Care / Marketing)
  [ ] Messaging consent: form submission ≠ SMS consent
  [ ] Message types listed (Customer Care / Marketing)
  [ ] Message frequency disclosed ("up to 4 msgs/month")
  [ ] Opt-out instructions (Reply STOP)
  [ ] Customer support contact (HELP keyword + email + phone)
  [ ] Message & data rate disclosure
  [ ] Carrier liability disclaimer
  [ ] Age restriction (18+)
  [ ] Governing law ([governing state])
  [ ] Cross-link to Privacy Policy

SECTION 4 — HIGHLEVEL STEP-BY-STEP REGISTRATION
================================================================

STEP 1 — VERIFY ALL THREE PAGES ARE LIVE
  Before touching HighLevel, open each URL in an incognito
  browser window and confirm they load publicly:

  [ ] [opt-in form URL]
      → Opt-in form visible, two checkboxes unchecked,
        Privacy Policy and Terms links in footer
  [ ] [privacy policy URL]
      → All required sections present, mobile info sharing
        statement present, link to Terms
  [ ] [terms URL]
      → All required clauses present, link to Privacy Policy

  ❌ Do NOT submit registration until all three pages pass.

STEP 2 — BRAND REGISTRATION
  GHL → Settings → Phone Numbers → A2P Registration
  Enter the following using values from the CLIENT INTAKE FORM
  (see Section 1 for exact field names):
    - Legal Company Name: → intake form "Legal Business Name"
    - Business Type:      → intake form "Business Entity Type"
    - EIN:                → intake form "EIN / Tax ID"
    - Business Address:   → intake form address fields
    - Website:            → intake form "Website URL"
    - Support Email:      → intake form "Support / Public Email"
    - Support Phone:      → intake form "Support / Public Phone"
  Submit brand registration and wait for approval.

STEP 3 — CAMPAIGN REGISTRATION
  After brand is approved:
    - Campaign Type: Customer Care + Marketing
    - Fill in and paste Campaign Description from Section 3
    - Opt-In Method: Website Form
    - Opt-In Form URL: [opt-in form URL]
    - Fill in and paste CTA / Message Flow from Section 3
    - Fill in and paste Sample Messages from Section 3
    - Set attributes to match the list in Section 3
  Submit campaign registration.

STEP 4 — POST-SUBMISSION CHECKS
  ❌ Do not remove STOP / HELP language from any page
  ❌ Do not change the opt-in form wording after submission
  ✅ All three pages must remain live throughout review
  ✅ Business address must match what is on file with the IRS
  ✅ Approval typically takes 2–4 business days
