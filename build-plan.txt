   - Service Areas dropdown → one link per area page
   - Blog
   - Contact
   - [PHONE]
   - "Get Free Quote" button (links to contact.html)

5. Build the FOOTER block (reusable snippet):
   - [HOURS]
   - [PHONE]
   - [EMAIL]
   - Links: all service pages, all area pages, Blog, Contact,
     Terms, Privacy Policy

6. Build SHARED SECTION BLOCKS (snippets, not files):
   - Quote Form: Full Name, Phone, Message, SMS checkbox, submit button
   - Reviews Section: heading + Google review CTA
   - Our Process Section: visual steps
